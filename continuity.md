## Up next

Ordered queue — top is next to pick up. Sequenced 2026-07-13: **ops safety first (1–3), the small outward seam (4), then the training-analytics arc in dependency order (5–12)**. Ordering constraints that matter: 5 must precede 6 (PMC is dishonest without per-sport TSS); 10 follows 5 (aligns TSS-estimate constants + reconciles the shared athlete-config consumption-gate delta); 11 follows 10 (both touch the race spec — avoid concurrent deltas); 12 goes last among the athlete-config changes so its ADDED-only history requirements merge cleanly after 5 and 10 archive.

1. **add-data-backup** — Postgres holds months of irreplaceable single-user history (meals/workouts/weight/coach-memory/plans) with **no backup or export path at all**; a disk failure or botched migration is unrecoverable. _Why now: protect the data before eight more changes touch schema and migrations._
2. **harden-server-baseline** — one-pass sweep of baseline operability gaps: boot-looping startup migration, no request deadline, unbounded bodies, 5xx-at-Info with no request id, zero metrics, MCP can't write athlete-config (the one REST↔MCP parity gap), the **known-red MCP E2E test** (`create_workout_template → not_found`), and untested `internal/numfmt`. _Why now: fixes the red E2E and gives every later change a clean baseline._
3. **harden-chat-turn-persistence** — the chat loop discards persistence errors at five sites in `internal/chat/service.go`; a failed persist silently loses the assistant turn or leaves malformed history (dangling `tool_use`) that the upstream API then rejects on every later request.
4. **public-race-feed** — Kazper's first (secret-gated) outward seam: a single curated **non-PII** read endpoint feeding a separate Strapi-backed public "road to race" countdown page. Strapi + the public frontend are out of scope here.
5. **add-per-sport-tss** — stored `tss` is Garmin's power-based value, so swim/run without power land NULL, making any future CTL/ATL/TSB dishonest; ingest-time rTSS/sTSS/hrTSS fallbacks with `tss_source` provenance + a re-runnable recompute endpoint; all needed thresholds already exist on athlete-config. _Why now: prerequisite for an honest PMC (6)._
6. **add-performance-management** — the Performance Management Chart: compute-on-read CTL/ATL/TSB daily series (Coggan 42/7-day EWMA) at `GET /performance/pmc` with ramp-rate alerts, `pmc_series` MCP tool, and a chart on the dashboard `/stats` page. No migration.
7. **add-intensity-distribution** — windowed time-in-zone distribution (the polarized/pyramidal check) aggregated from the per-workout `secs_in_zone_*` columns; extends the `workout-stats` capability (`GET /workouts/intensity-distribution`), no migration, no new wiring. _Cheap read-side win right after PMC lands on `/stats`._
8. **persist-activity-streams** — retain the raw 1 Hz samples that `POST /workouts/{id}/streams` currently discards: new `workout_streams` table (`REAL[]`), HR stream added to the bridge, VI/EF/aerobic-decoupling stored on the workout row. _(supersedes the strava-stats "raw-stream retention" deferral.)_
9. **add-step-compliance** — per-step *execution* compliance ("interval 3 was 20W under target") at `GET /workouts/{id}/compliance`: strict lap↔step matching against the training-plan effective program, honest `unavailable` on mismatch. New leaf package, no migration.
10. **add-race-pacing-plan** — deterministic per-leg race pacing (duration-banded %FTP / threshold-pace / CSS targets + leg TSS estimates) as the sibling of per-leg fuelling, with persisted `(race_id, ordinal)` overrides. _After 5: aligns TSS constants and reconciles the shared athlete-config delta._
11. **add-race-priority** — A/B/C priority on the race resource (advisory metadata the coach agent reasons over; no hard macrocycle coupling in v1). _Tiny; after 10 to avoid concurrent race-spec deltas._
12. **add-threshold-history** — versioned athlete-config thresholds (dated snapshots on threshold-changing PUTs, `GET /athlete-config/history`, an as-of lookup for future consumers) so "FTP went 240→255→270 this season" is answerable. _Last of the athlete-config deltas — merges after 5 and 10 archive._
